About Kedarnath

Kedarnath is one of the holiest Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva, located in the Garhwal Himalayan range in Uttarakhand, India. It is a revered destination for pilgrims and adventure seekers alike. Nestled in snow-covered peaks, the temple offers spiritual bliss and a breathtaking view of the Himalayas. This guide will help you plan your trip with essential information about the journey, services, prices, and more.
